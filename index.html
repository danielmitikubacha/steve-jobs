Steve Jobs

who is American businessman, inventor, founder and CEO of apple company.

Steve Jobs short life history

  • Born- Steven Paul Job February 24, 1955 San Francisco, California, US
  • Education- Reed College (attended)
  • Known for-Co-creator of the Apple II, Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, iPad, and first Apple Stores
  • Title-Co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc.
    -Primary investor and chairman of Pixar
    -Founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT
  • Spouse-Laurene Powell (m. 1991)
  • Partner-Chrisann Brennan
  • Awards-Presidential Medal of Freedom (posthumous, 2022)
